Welcome to MemeCoin!

A fun way to create and stake meme tokens on Shibarium with WoofSwap

What is MemeCoin?

MemeCoin lets you create your own meme-themed token and earn rewards by staking. Built on Shibarium, it’s fast, cheap, and works exclusively with WoofSwap liquidity pools. Whether you’re a creator or a staker, enjoy the fun of growing your tokens!

How It Works

  • Create Your Token: Design a meme token with a unique name and symbol.
  • Stake to Earn: Stake WoofSwap LP tokens or MemeCoin for rewards.
  • Collect Fees: Creators earn fees from staking activity.
  • Rewards Over Time: Rewards start high and taper off until the supply is exhausted.

Token Details

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000,000,000 (1 quadrillion) tokens.
  • Staking Units:
    • WoofSwap LP: Custom unit set at creation (e.g., 1,000,000 wei).
    • MemeCoin: 10,000 tokens per unit.
  • Mining Ends: When 1 quadrillion tokens are minted.

Reward Rules

  • WoofSwap LP Rewards:
    • Initial: 500 tokens per block per unit.
    • Minimum: 10 tokens per block per unit.
  • MemeCoin Rewards:
    • Initial: 0.025 tokens per block per unit.
    • Minimum: 0.0005 tokens per block per unit.
  • Decay Rate: Decreases 2% every 600,000 blocks (~1 month).
  • Max Duration: ~5 years (29,400,000 blocks).

How to Create Your MemeCoin

Use our Shibarium app:

  • Connect a wallet (e.g., MetaMask) with BONE for gas.
  • Select “Deploy MemeCoin.”
  • Enter:
    • Name: e.g., “DogCoin”
    • Symbol: e.g., “DOG”
    • WoofSwap LP Address: From WoofSwap.
    • Stake Unit LP: Minimum LP per stake (e.g., 1,000,000 wei).
  • Click “Deploy” and confirm.
  • Get your token address from the app.

As the creator, you’ll collect staking fees!

How to Stake and Earn

Stake with any MemeCoin:

  • Stake WoofSwap LP:
    • Search the MemeCoin address.
    • Input LP amount (in stake unit multiples).
    • Approve and stake.
  • Stake MemeCoin:
    • Search the MemeCoin address.
    • Input amount (multiples of 10,000).
    • Stake.
  • Claim Rewards: Claim anytime.
  • Unstake: Withdraw all with rewards.

Fees for Creators

Creators receive WoofSwap LP pool fees automatically when users stake or unstake LP tokens.

Safety Features

  • Secure staking with no reentrancy risks.
  • Fees restricted to the creator.
  • Hard cap at 1 quadrillion tokens.
