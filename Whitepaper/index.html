Welcome to MemeCoin!

A fun way to create and stake meme tokens on Shibarium with WoofSwap

What is MemeCoin?

MemeCoin lets you create your own meme-themed token and earn rewards by staking. It runs on Shibarium, a fast and cheap blockchain, and works only with WoofSwap liquidity pools. Whether you’re a creator or a staker, it’s all about having fun and growing your tokens!

How It Works

  • Create Your Token: Make a meme token with a name and symbol you love.
  • Stake to Earn: Stake WoofSwap LP tokens or your MemeCoin to get rewards.
  • Collect Fees: If you create a token, you earn fees from stakers.
  • Rewards Over Time: Rewards start high and decrease slowly until they stop.

Token Details

  • Total Tokens: Up to 1,000,000,000,000,000 (1 quadrillion).
  • Staking Units:
    • WoofSwap LP: Set when you create the token (e.g., 1,000,000 tiny units).
    • MemeCoin: 10,000 tokens per stake.
  • Rewards Stop: When all 1 quadrillion tokens are made.

Reward Rules

  • WoofSwap LP Rewards:
    • Start at 500 tokens per block per stake unit.
    • Drop to 10 tokens minimum over time.
  • MemeCoin Rewards:
    • Start at 0.025 tokens per block per stake unit.
    • Drop to 0.0005 tokens minimum over time.
  • Reward Decrease: Drops 2% every month (600,000 blocks).
  • Max Time: Rewards run for about 5 years (29,400,000 blocks).
  • Max Claim: 1,000,000 tokens per claim.

How to Create Your MemeCoin

Use our easy app on Shibarium:

  • Connect your wallet (like MetaMask) with some BONE for fees.
  • Go to “Deploy MemeCoin.”
  • Fill in:
    • Name: e.g., “CatCoin”
    • Symbol: e.g., “CAT”
    • WoofSwap LP Address: Get this from WoofSwap.
    • Stake Unit LP: Minimum LP to stake (e.g., 1,000,000).
  • Click “Deploy” and confirm in your wallet.
  • Find your token address in the app afterward.

You’ll own the token and collect fees from stakers!

How to Stake and Earn

Join the fun with any MemeCoin:

  • Stake WoofSwap LP:
    • Search the MemeCoin address in the app.
    • Enter your WoofSwap LP amount (must match the stake unit).
    • Approve and stake it.
  • Stake MemeCoin:
    • Search the MemeCoin address.
    • Enter your amount (multiples of 10,000 tokens).
    • Stake it.
  • Claim Rewards: Click “Claim Rewards” anytime.
  • Unstake: Click “Unstake All” to get your tokens back plus rewards.

Fees for Creators

If you made the MemeCoin, you get fees from the WoofSwap LP pool every time someone stakes or unstakes LP tokens. These go straight to your wallet!

Safety Features

  • No sneaky tricks allowed (safe staking).
  • Only you can collect fees if you’re the creator.
  • Tokens stop at 1 quadrillion—no surprises.
